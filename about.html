My Story

I'm a (self-proclaimed) jack of all trades who enjoys sketching (the cure to all life's ailments), designing of the graphic or web variety (ditto), and the occasional animating. Maybe you want to collaborate on a video game? I've been partaking in some C# programming as well as designing characters for my own personal video game I've been working on.

With any luck, that will be on the app store in the next few months.

I'm also the current Chief Design Officer of BoatHouse Pictures, a film production company located in Philadephia, Pennsylvania. My home away from home, considering I'm also a giant film buff on top of a design nerd.
